About Us

Our Story

We're a sincere company with a straight forward vision. We believe that there is an amazing amount to be learned by working in a "real world" productive enviorment. Along side other like minded nerds. With this in mind we began to gather innovative, creative, stylish ideas in Febuary 2018. From there the company quickly expanded to a fully productive team, and we decided on the name "HARVEL ELECTRIC". After a slow start and many hurdles, we found three solid dedicated members who lead the team today.

What We Bring to the table today

We've had an amazing journey coming to where we are today, and now feel confident that we can provide you the luxuries and comforts of a modern life in the most modern and stylish manner, neccesities as they are known for us today. We have extensive products and a vast variety for you to choose from. Products that meet your every day need in the most chic way possible.
